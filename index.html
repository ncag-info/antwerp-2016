Description

The aim of the conference is to bring together experts and graduate students/postdocs working in various modern approaches to geometry. Example topics would be:
  • derived algebraic / symplectic geometry
  • derived minimal model program
  • homological projective duality
  • homological mirror symmetry
  • stability conditions
  • non-commutative motives / non-commutative (co)homology theories
  • generalizations of derived categories / cDG-categories / matrix factorizations
  • operads / graph complexes / deformation theory
  • non-commutative versions of classical varieties (del Pezzo, tori, K3, ...)

Time and venue

The conference starts on Monday, September 19 in the morning and ends on Saturday, September 24 at noon.

Registration, financial support and accomodation

Registration is compulsory. The deadline for registration if you wish to be considered for financial support is May 1.

Financial support can be applied for when registering.

For funded participants rooms including breakfast will be booked by the organisers based on the information given via registration. All other participants are asked to contact a hotel directly. A list of hotels will be provided soon.
